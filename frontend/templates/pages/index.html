Your simple, focused CRM to manage relationships without the enterprise bloat.

The Problem

Most CRMs are built for massive teams with endless features you’ll never use. If you're a solo founder or small team, it just gets in your way.

The Solution

MiniHubspot brings you the essentials: contact management, email verification, and integration with Stripe — all in a clean, server-side rendered app that doesn’t slow you down.

  • ✅ Lightweight and fast
  • ✅ No JavaScript frameworks required
  • ✅ Secure, backend-focused architecture

Who It's For

If you're a:

  • 🔹 Solo founder managing early customer relationships
  • 🔹 Freelancer building a lightweight client database
  • 🔹 Developer tired of bloated SaaS tools

Built with Go, SQLC, Stripe, and ❤️ — by a backend dev who loves simplicity.
